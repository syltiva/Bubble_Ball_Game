BUBBLE BALL

Tired? Is your back breaking from coding all day? Does your brain feel bogged down from debugging? Take a couple minutes to escape into an underwater oasis and venture into some blissful, beaming, bubbly, badass-ness. The game is simple:

goal: get your ball onto the target platform

to play:
< -- left arrow propels the ball to the left
> -- right arrow propels the ball to the right
^ -- up arrow propels the ball upward

You can only move the ball when it is in the shaded paths of where the propellers point. Maneuver around the bubbles to get to the platform and win the game! There's no score or times limit-- so relax and take a break with Bubble Ball!
